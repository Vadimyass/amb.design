01 / About

A little about me

My name is Anastasia, and I'm a graphic designer specializing in logo creation, brand identity development, and packaging design for your brand. I would be happy to help you create a unique visual identity that is both modern and memorable.

Based in Odesa, Ukraine, I work with clients worldwide to bring their visions to life. Every project is a chance to tell a story through design — from elegant cosmetic clinics to vibrant flower shops and bold fashion labels.

I use Figma, Adobe Illustrator, and Photoshop to craft every detail with precision and care. Reach out to me, and we'll make your project stand out.

Logo Design
